Digital Artworks

I enjoy working in the Adobe Suite. Here are some digital artworks I created in my spare time, over the years:


Fuzzy Fish Creature

This is a fish creature, I made in photoshop. From a photo I took of a peacock feather and various photoshop tools, I created a mystical creature.



Why Research Matters

This was an entry piece for a contest. The challenge was to create a piece that answers the question, "Why research matters?". The winner got a ticket to the 2019 Strive UX Research Conference. In my piece, I choose to create an illustration of a puzzle to represent that without research, things would not work and pieces would not match up. Without it, the project will fall apart because the information and knowledge is not there.
Sidenote: I end up winning the ticket. :)




Fish Tank

This is my fish tank that I drew over the summer. I draw my tank each summer to keep track of the fishes over the years.



Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow

I chose to create this piece to communicate the message, do not dwell on the past. You can't change what you did, so just enjoy the present and make the most out of your future.




Ninja Me

This fun cartoon was done in my spare time. The illustration is of myself drawn as a ninja from the hidden leaf village.




ITooPlant Graphic

This is a graphic that I had made as a volunteer project for an environmental company called ITooPlant, located in Mumbai, India. The birds are Asian flycatchers and the tree is a fig tree. They represent the need to nurture Asia's population. The resources in Asia may not be able to support the growing population.



Golden Paws App Icon

These are some app icons I made for a fictional dog clinic. A variety was created to explore different options.
