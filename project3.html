Digital Artworks

Here are some digital artworks I created in my spare time, over the years:


Why Research Matters

This was an entry to a contest. The challenge was to create a piece that answers the question, "Why research matters?". The winner got a ticket to the 2019 UX strive conference. In my piece, I choose to create an illustration of a puzzle to represent that without research, things would not work and pieces would not match up. I believe that research such as looking at past projects, doing user research and competitor analysis creates the foundation of the project. Without it, the project will fall apart because the information and knowledge is not there.
Sidenote: I end up winning the ticket. :)



ITooPlant Graphic

This is a graphic that I had made for an environmental company called ITooPlant, located in Mumbai, India. The birds are Asian flycatchers and the tree is a fig tree. They represent the need to nurture Asia's population. The resources in Asia may not be able to support the growing population.



Fish Creature

This is a fish creature, I made in photoshop. From a photo I took of a peacock feather and various photoshop tools, I created a mystical creature.



Fish Tank

This is my fish tank I drew over the summer. I like to draw my tank each summer to keep track of the fishes over the years.



Yesterday Today Tomorrow

I chose to create this piece to communicate the message, do not dwell on the past. You can't change what you did, so just enjoy the present and make the most out of your future.
